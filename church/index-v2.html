Welcome to Living Word Temple of Restoration

It is our great privilege to have you fellowship with us today. It is our prayer that you will find us to be a loving and hospitable community of believers committed to the Lord Jesus Christ.In our worship, we seek to be open to the leading and work of the Holy Spirit. We are an expressive church that enjoys praising and worshiping God. This flows out of a commitment to be faithful in our teaching and studying of God's Word, along with applying its' truth to our daily lives.
