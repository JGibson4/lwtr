History of Ministry

John Miller is an ordained minister with Elim Fellowship of Lima, NY. He has a degree from Elim Bible Institute, a B.Div. from Trinity Seminary, CPE from the University of Rochester, a M.Div. from Northeastern Seminary, and a Ph.D. in Renewal Studies from Regent University. Dr. Miller has authored four books; he has contributed eight articles to three encyclopedic volumes and has published many academic journal articles.

Dr. Miller is an adjunct professor for Regent University, a facilitator for Northeastern Seminary, and a teacher with the Sound of the Genuine Institute. He serves as the Academic Dean and as an instructor at Elim Bible Institute; likewise, his wife (Virginia) serves Elim as the Dean of Women. Dr. Miller serves as the Pastor of Education for Living Word Temple of Restoration, where he enjoys teaching teachers and helping every member find an opportunity to become involved in ministry. He and his wife have four adult children and four grandchildren.
