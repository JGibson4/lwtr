Rev. Thomas C. Linder & Rev. Regina Linder

HomePastor Linder

History of Ministry

Rev. Thomas Linder’s educational background is in Business Administration. He has been serving as Executive Pastor at Living Word Temple of Restoration for 16 years. He was born and raised in Rochester, NY. He oversees various auxiliaries at Living Word that include the finance and worship teams. He and his wife Regina have 3 beautiful children. He has been employed with Applied Coatings for over 21 years and currently oversees the Material Preparation Department. Pastor Linder has a passion for ministering to families through supporting healthy marriages through the couple’s ministry. He also assists with outreach by sharing the gospel with the local community. In general he labors to see the body of Christ walk in its full potential.
