  • Senior Pastor Bishop W. R. Turner Jr. And First Lady Ruth B. Turner

    Sed ornare vestibulum mauris nec

    Bishop William R. Turner serves Living Word Temple of Restoration in a full-time capacity. Bishop Turner is a native of the Rochester area where he resides with his wife Ruth and their three children. He received his Bachelor of Arts degree in Sociology in 1998, a Masters of Theology in 2002 from Northeastern Seminary, and is currently a Doctoral student in the Counseling and Human Development program at the University of Rochester. He has been in the ministry for over 20 years. He has been a youth pastor for Faith Temple of the Living God Church Rochester, New York 1988 to 1996, and Chaplain at Nazareth College of Rochester from 1994 to 1997. He also served as the Interim Pastor for Shiloh Missionary Baptist Church, also located in Rochester, New York from 1995 to 1996.

    Rev. Thomas Linder’s educational background is in Business Administration. He has been serving as Executive Pastor at Living Word Temple of Restoration for 16 years. He was born and raised in Rochester, NY. He oversees various auxiliaries at Living Word that include the finance and worship teams. He and his wife Regina have 3 beautiful children. He has been employed with Applied Coatings for over 21 years and currently oversees the Material Preparation Department. Pastor Linder has a passion for ministering to families through supporting healthy marriages through the couple’s ministry. He also assists with outreach by sharing the gospel with the local community. In general he labors to see the body of Christ walk in its full potential.
